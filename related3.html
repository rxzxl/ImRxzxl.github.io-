Trip To Perhentian

Last semester, I went on a trip to Pulau Perhentian with Mus and Ikmal for three days and two nights. The journey was filled with unforgettable experiences. Pulau Perhentian offered breathtaking views, with crystal-clear waters and beautiful beaches. Throughout the trip, we enjoyed snorkeling, which introduced us to the stunning coral reefs and fascinating marine life. We also took the time to explore the island, try the delicious local food, and soak in the peaceful natural surroundings. Every moment spent with Mus and Ikmal created sweet memories, filled with tranquility and the beauty of the environment. This picture captures the unforgettable moments we had on Pulau Perhentian, a place full of friendship and endless joy.
